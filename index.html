SHARP

Saving Historic Areas for Revitalization and Prosperity

SHARP is a North Carolina nonprofit organization focused on historic preservation, adaptive reuse, and community revitalization in underserved communities.

Our mission is to preserve architectural heritage, stimulate local economic growth, encourage sustainable rehabilitation practices, and reinvest in historic communities throughout Eastern North Carolina.

Current Status

SHARP is currently organizing operations and pursuing federal 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status.
